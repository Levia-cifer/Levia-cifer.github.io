01 / ABOUT

I'm a Business IT student at UPSA in Accra, spending equal time in lecture halls and in the terminal. Most of what I build starts as a personal itch — a bot to run a game with friends, a way to survive exam season, a booking site for hostels near campus — and turns into something I keep iterating on long after the original problem is solved.

I like tools that are opinionated but simple: something a non-technical friend can pick up in two minutes. That bias shows up in everything from my code to how I write documentation.
